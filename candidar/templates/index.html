ABOUT

About Us - CandiDar

CandiDar is a Moroccan platform offering unlimited interview practice. Inspired by the concept of home, a place of safety and support in Moroccan culture, CandiDar combines Candi (Candidate) and Dar (home in Arabic). We provide a nurturing space where candidates can practice, learn from mistakes, and build confidence at their own pace—anytime, anywhere.

Our vision

To empower Moroccan graduates by offering a platform for mock interviews and constructive feedback, helping them secure their dream job amidst the challenges of the Moroccan job market.
